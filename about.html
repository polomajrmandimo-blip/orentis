Our Story

Orentis was founded on a simple conviction: that legal language should not be a barrier between the law and the people it affects. South Africa's regulatory and commercial environment is sophisticated and dynamic — but the documentation that moves through it is often dense, opaque, and inaccessible even to the executives and professionals it is meant to serve.

We work at the intersection of law, language, and communication. Our clients are corporate treasuries, banking institutions, asset managers, and private equity funds operating in South Africa and across the continent. What they share is a need for precision: documents, communications, and content that say exactly what they mean — clearly, without ambiguity, and without burying the reader in unnecessary complexity.

South Africa's post-apartheid legal order is built on the premise that law should be accessible to all. Yet in practice, the volume and technical density of commercial and regulatory documentation has made that aspiration difficult to achieve. Orentis exists to close that gap. We believe that a well-drafted, plain-language document is not a simplification of the law — it is the law, expressed with the skill and care it deserves.

We are a lean, senior team. We do not subcontract. Every engagement is handled by experienced practitioners who understand both the legal substance and the communication challenge. Our clients come to us because they need the work done properly, not quickly and cheaply. We deliver it properly, and we deliver it on time.

"We are not a law firm, and we are not a translation agency. We are something more useful: communicators who understand the law, and lawyers who understand communication."

Our Values

What we stand for

  • Precision

    We do not approximate. Every word in a legal or commercial document carries consequence, and we treat it accordingly. Our work is drafted, reviewed, and refined until it says exactly what it must — no more, no less.

  • Clarity

    Clarity is not the absence of complexity — it is the mastery of it. We take technically demanding material and make it genuinely comprehensible to its intended audience, without stripping away the rigour that makes it reliable.

  • Independence

    We have no affiliations with law firms, financial institutions, or advisory groups that could compromise the objectivity of our work. Our clients retain us for our judgement, and that judgement remains independent.

  • South African Focus

    We are specialists in South African law, regulation, and commercial practice. We understand the Companies Act, the FSCA regulatory framework, Exchange Control, the FAIS Act, and the specific dynamics of doing business in this market — because it is the only market we serve.
